Welcome To the colors application at KZNSC

Make your application to represent with pride to belong

On this platform kindly submit your Colours Application in order to represent Kwa Zulu Natal Province in your respective Sporting Code at your National Event

Before taking on the application, please take note of the following

  • The application may only be submitted by Provincial Federation, composite and multi-sport bodies who are members of the KZNSC.

  • Before completing this form, applicants must study the documents RULES AND PROCEDURES APPLICABLE TO THE AWARD OF PROVINCIAL COLORS carefully.

  • Applications must be submitted at least 30 days prior to the departure date of the team/commencement date of the event.

  • The following documents must accompany the application:
    * Letter of invitation from host province/national federation, confirming the status, nature, and program of the event.
    * Copies of South African identity document/passports of athletes as proof of South African citizenship.

  • KZNSC Approval for participation in the event must be obtained from the KZNSC before submitting this application (KZNSC - PC2).
